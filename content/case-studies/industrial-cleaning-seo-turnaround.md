## The situation

The client is an online supplier of professional-grade cleaning and detailing products for trucking, automotive, and marine industries — soaps, degreasers, foam cannons, and restoration products built for heavy-duty performance. The product lineup was strong and the niche was clear. The problem was that almost none of it showed up in search.

Buyers searching for truck cleaning products, heavy-duty acid cleaners, or foam cannons had no reliable way of finding the site. Before this engagement, most of the retailer's priority keywords weren't ranking in the first 100 results at all. Its partner agency needed a search team who could fix that quietly, under its own name, with the retailer never needing to know a second agency was involved.

## The challenge

Three structural gaps were capping the site's visibility before any content or backlink work could matter:

- **No dedicated category pages.** There were no SEO-focused landing pages for boat cleaning, RV cleaning, pontoon cleaning, or car and truck cleaning — high-intent categories with real search demand and nowhere on the site built to capture it.
- **No blog or educational content.** With no content strategy in place, the site had no way to reach buyers still researching — the informational searches that build trust long before a purchase.
- **Under-optimized existing pages.** Several live category and product pages were missing basic metadata, clear keyword targeting, and structured internal linking, quietly capping the performance of pages that were already indexed.

## The approach

Five workstreams ran in parallel, each targeting a different part of the visibility gap.

![Five workstreams run in parallel: keyword research, on-page optimization, technical SEO, content strategy, and link building](/images/case-studies/cleaning-seo/strategy-overview.svg)

Keyword research targeted a deliberate mix of short and long-tail terms — truck cleaning products, car cleaning supplies, heavy-duty acid cleaners, foam cannons — chosen to capture both broad category traffic and specific, high-intent buyer searches.

On-page work rebuilt meta titles, descriptions, and header tags around those keywords, then rewrote product and category page content for relevance and readability rather than keyword stuffing. Internal linking was strengthened throughout to help authority flow to the pages that needed it most and make the full catalog easier to crawl.

The technical SEO pass resolved sitewide issues — slow page speed, broken links — and layered in structured data (schema markup) to improve the site's eligibility for rich results in search.

Content strategy filled the gap the retailer had no answer for: a content plan built around truck washing, boat and RV cleaning, detailing technique guides, and product usage content, positioning the brand as a trusted authority rather than just another product listing.

Link building closed the loop with a mix of citations, bookmarking, and image-sharing activity, plus direct outreach to industry-relevant sites and partners to secure high-quality guest post backlinks and build durable domain authority.

## The results

Every workstream showed up in the metrics within six months.

![Results dashboard showing organic traffic up 250%, organic revenue up 400%, referring domains up 40%, domain rating up 5 points, top-10 rankings up 500%, and total keywords in SERP up 216%](/images/case-studies/cleaning-seo/results-dashboard.svg)

Organic traffic grew from roughly 2,000 to over 7,000 monthly visits — a 250% increase. Keywords ranking in the top 10 grew from just 1 to 6, a 500% jump, while total keywords appearing anywhere in search results climbed from 6 to 19 — a 216% increase in overall visibility. Referring domains grew from 57 to over 80, a 40% increase in the site's backlink footprint, and domain rating improved from 2 to 7, a meaningful early jump in search engine trust for a site that was starting from close to zero authority.

The number that mattered most to the client's bottom line: organic revenue grew from $4.5K to over $25K per month — a 400% increase — as better rankings converted into buyers who were actually ready to purchase, not just browse.

## What this proves for a white-label partner

This engagement started from a genuinely difficult position: a site with almost no search visibility, no content engine, and category pages that didn't exist yet. Building category-specific landing pages, fixing the technical foundation, and pairing that with a deliberate content and link-building push turned that into 6x organic revenue growth in a single two-quarter window — without the retailer ever needing to know a second agency was behind the work.

For a partner agency, that's the pitch in practice: hand off the SEO account, keep the client relationship, and let results like these carry your name.

## The situation

A manufacturing ecommerce brand selling across multiple product categories needed top-of-funnel Meta campaigns launched simultaneously across several distinct product lines, paired with lifecycle email automation to capture and nurture leads post-click.

## The approach

- Structured TOF campaigns by product category rather than a single blended campaign
- Built category-specific creative testing cycles to isolate what messaging worked per product line
- Connected Klaviyo flows to capture and nurture leads generated from paid traffic
- Set up HubSpot automation to route and qualify leads before they reached the sales team
